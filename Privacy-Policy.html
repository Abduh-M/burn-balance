Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

Burn Balance is built with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal data with anyone. Everything stays on your device.

Information You Enter

To calculate your TDEE, you provide:

  • Body metrics — height, weight, age, sex
  • Daily activity data — step count, cardio details, calories consumed
  • Training information — workout type and intensity

All of this information is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It is never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage

Burn Balance stores all your data locally on your device using AsyncStorage — a standard mobile local storage mechanism. This means:

  • Your data exists only on your phone
  • We have no access to your data at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data
  • No account or login is required

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising data
  • No health data synced to any server

Third-Party Services

Burn Balance does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app works fully offline and requires no internet connection to function.

The app is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google's own privacy policy governs any data collected through the Play Store platform itself — this is outside our control and unrelated to Burn Balance's functionality.

Health Disclaimer

Burn Balance provides estimated calorie calculations based on established fitness formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor, MET-based models). These are approximations, not medical advice.

We recommend tracking your steps and activity using a smartwatch or fitness tracker for the most accurate results. Always consult a healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet or exercise routine.

Children's Privacy

Burn Balance is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app and provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
